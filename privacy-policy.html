Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

Privacy First: BrightMinds does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your activities, progress, and preferences stay locally on your device. You maintain complete privacy and control over your information.

1. Overview

BrightMinds ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data and privacy for the BrightMinds mobile application.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

BrightMinds is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics data
  • Quiz answers or performance data
  • User preferences or settings
  • Any other personally identifiable information

3. Local Data Storage

All data related to your use of BrightMinds is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Your quiz progress and completion history
  • Answer history and performance tracking
  • App preferences and settings
  • Bookmarked questions or content

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it using the app's sharing features.

4. User-Initiated Sharing

BrightMinds includes optional sharing features that allow you to share your achievements or content with others. When you use these features:

  • Sharing only occurs when you explicitly initiate it
  • You control exactly what information is shared
  • Sharing is handled through your device's native sharing capabilities
  • We do not receive, store, or process any shared content

5. Third-Party Services

BrightMinds does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection for core functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, BrightMinds is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, regardless of age.

7. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data security is protected by your device's security measures
  • We recommend using your device's built-in security features (passcode, biometric authentication, etc.)
  • If you uninstall the app, all local data will be removed from your device

8. Internet Permissions

BrightMinds does not require internet access for its core functionality. If the app requests internet permissions, it is solely for:

  • Optional content updates (if implemented in future versions)
  • Sharing features that use your device's sharing capabilities

No personal data is transmitted even when internet access is available.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete
  • You have complete control over all app data stored on your device
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. If we ever introduce features that collect personal data, we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy accordingly
  • Make it clear and transparent what data is collected and why
  • Provide options to opt-out or control data collection
  • Notify users through the app

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Website: brightminds.today
Email: privacy@brightminds.today

12. Consent

By using BrightMinds, you consent to this Privacy Policy. Since we do not collect personal data, your use of the app does not involve the processing of personal information.

Bottom Line: BrightMinds respects your privacy completely. What happens in the app stays in the app. Your data is yours alone.
