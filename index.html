Sharpen Your Mind with BrightMinds

BrightMinds combines the intellectual rigor of LSAT-style logic questions with the timeless wisdom of Biblical narratives. Each day, you'll receive 7-12 carefully crafted multiple-choice questions that challenge your critical thinking while deepening your understanding of Scripture.

Whether you're preparing for standardized tests, want to strengthen your reasoning skills, or simply enjoy the challenge of logical puzzles rooted in meaningful stories, BrightMinds offers a unique daily practice that exercises both mind and spirit.
